## 2026 ALGORITHM RULES — APPLY TO ALL CHANNELS

### Hard Rules
1. NEVER open with "Hey guys welcome back" — instant drop-off
2. Script content must match title/description exactly — YouTube AI reads captions; mismatch kills suggested traffic
3. Never pad content to hit 8 minutes — retention loss > mid-roll gain
4. Never put the only CTA at the end — only 16% reach the final 10%

### Hook Structure (First 30 Seconds)
```
0:00–0:05  GRAB    — bold claim or unexpected moment
0:05–0:15  PROMISE — exact outcome viewer gets from watching
0:15–0:30  STAKES  — why it matters, what they lose by leaving
```
Target: 70%+ retention at 30 seconds

### Retention Engineering
- Pattern interrupt every 60–90s: [CAMERA CHANGE] [B-ROLL CUE] [GRAPHIC] [SOUND EFFECT] [UNEXPECTED STAT]
- Soft CTA at ~1 min (60% of viewers still watching)
- Open loop mid-video — suspension bridge = 68% higher completion
- Retention re-hook at ~60% mark — second hook moment
- Hard CTA in outro — maintain energy to last second, no wind-down

### Retention Benchmarks
| Metric | Target | Status |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Retention at 30s | 70%+ | Solid hook |
| Retention at 30s | 80%+ | Exceptional |
| AVD | 70%+ | Viral zone — priority push |
| AVD | 50–60% | Solid |
| Only 16% | reach final 10% | Never CTA-only at end |
| 8 min mark | unlocks mid-roll | ~50% more revenue |

### 2026 Platform Updates
- YouTube now shows every upload to strangers first — subscriber count irrelevant at upload time
- Only question the algorithm asks: "Does this video satisfy this viewer right now?"
- YouTube's AI reads auto-captions — script must match title/description or suggested traffic dies

## SCRIPT FRAMEWORK — AgriciDaniel/claude-youtube

Full structure for every script:

```
## HOOK (0:00–0:30)
  Grab (0–5s): single most important line
  Promise (5–15s): specific tangible outcome
  Stakes (15–30s): emotional/practical consequence of leaving

## INTRO (0:30–2:00): Context + credibility

## CONTENT BLOCKS (2:00+):
  [PATTERN INTERRUPT] → content → [B-ROLL CUE] → micro-summary → forward hook
  Repeat with new interrupt every 60–90s

## SOFT CTA (~1 min mark)

## OPEN LOOP (mid-video): tease something for later

## RETENTION RE-HOOK (~60% mark)

## OUTRO: hard CTA — energy to last second
```

**Always include at end of script:**
- Pattern Interrupt Log (timestamp + type + description)
- Retention Risk Map (timestamp + risk level + mitigation)
- Editor Notes ([VISUAL] and [SFX] cues for Whisk/Hunyuan)

## AB TEST FRAMEWORK — EVERY VIDEO

Always generate 3 psychologically distinct title variants. Never test similar titles.

| Variant | Trigger | Pattern |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Curiosity | Unknown/secret/unsolved | "The Heist Nobody Can Explain" |
| Fear/Loss | Gone/destroyed/stolen/too late | "The $500M That Disappeared Before Anyone Noticed" |
| Result/Number | Specific $, time, count | "2 Men Stole $500M in 81 Minutes" |

When YouTube picks the winner → that reveals your audience's dominant psychological trigger. Apply to all future videos for that channel.
